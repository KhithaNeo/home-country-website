A little bit about the city of gold...

South Africa is the southern most country on the continent of Africa and has eleven official languages which include English, Zulu, Sotho and Xhosa. Xhosa happens to be my home language. I come from Johannesburg, a culturally diverse metropolis that is largely considered the economic hub of the country. Interestingly, a Joburg suburb called Sandton is said to be home to the wealthiest square mile on the continent.
